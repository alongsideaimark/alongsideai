Questions

Fair things to ask before saying yes.

I don't know anything about AI. Is that okay? +

That's exactly who we're for. Most of our clients have heard of ChatGPT and not much else. You don't need to know anything going in. You don't need to learn jargon. You answer a few questions about how you work; we handle the rest.

Why a questionnaire first, instead of a phone call? +

Because you shouldn't have to explain your life to a stranger on the phone to figure out if we can help. Answer the questions at your own pace, on your own time. We'll send back a plan. If it sounds right, then we talk. If it doesn't, you've lost nothing and you keep the plan.

Will you try to sell me a bunch of software? +

No. We make nothing from software companies. We always check what you already pay for first — most people are surprised how much is already covered. When we do recommend something new, it's because the math works for you, not us.

What if I get stuck after you leave? +

Every engagement includes a 30-day check-in where we come back and sort out whatever came up. After that, some clients stay on a small monthly retainer — your AI person on speed dial — and some don't. Either is fine.

Is this only for business owners? +

Not at all. About half our clients are retirees or people nearing retirement who want to travel more, stay organized, or finally feel in control of their devices. The Personal tiers are built for exactly that.

How long does the whole thing take? +

The questionnaire takes about 10 minutes. Your plan arrives within two business days. If you decide to continue, the setup-and-training portion is about a week, split across short sessions. Then we come back at day 30.

Do you work in person or over video? +

Both. In-person in the Greater Los Angeles area; over video anywhere else. Most clients do a mix — the first visit in person, training over video once we know each other.
