**Is there anything you avoid doing because it's confusing or tedious?**

Going through the QuickBooks reports. I let the office gal handle that and I look at the numbers she prints out for me. Anything inside that program I do not touch.

**What do you wish someone would just handle for you?**

Customer billing reminders. We've got maintenance accounts that pay 30 days late routinely and the office gal sends invoices but doesn't always follow up. I lose maybe $40K a year in things that just kind of fade. That's real money.

**Have you ever thought "there has to be a better way to do this"? What was it about?**

Hiring crew. Fellas come and go. We've used Indeed, we've used the local paper still believe it or not, we've gotten guys from other companies. I don't know what he's talking about exactly but he's probably right.
